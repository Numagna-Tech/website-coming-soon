Signal Sentinel: Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Signal Sentinel (developed by Numagna Technologies) handles your data.

1. Permissions & Data Collection

Signal Sentinel uses the following permissions to provide network diagnostic features:

  • INTERNET: To verify active connectivity.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To monitor signal strength and carrier information.

Note: No personal identification data (name, email, phone number, or account info) is collected, stored, or transmitted by this application.

2. Future Location Features

Future versions may request Location Access to enable dead-zone mapping. This data will be processed locally on your device and will not be shared with Numagna Technologies unless explicitly authorized by you.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Because Signal Sentinel does not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no user data for us to retain or delete. Any diagnostic data generated by the app remains on your local device and is cleared if the app is uninstalled.

4. Children’s Privacy

Signal Sentinel does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our services are not directed at children.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Your signal data stays on your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

7. Contact Us

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact: support@numagnatech.com
